Buku

     Tobacco is an agricultural product that utilizes the leaves to be processed into pesticides, drugs, and cigarettes. The quality of tobacco is determined by the maintenance of plants and the reduction of pests and diseases. To avoid such disturbances, the need for fast, precise and accurate controls in order for tobacco plant diseases can not spread throughout the farmland. In the manufacture of fuzzy, diseases and symptoms in tobacco plants used as a rule base in the manufacture of Expert Fuzzy System.
